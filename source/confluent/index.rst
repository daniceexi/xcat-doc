Confluent 
=========

What is Confluent
-----------------

Confluent is a new codebase with a few goals in mind:

* Augment xCAT 2.X series 
* Potentially serve in place of xCAT-server for the next generation of xCAT

**Disclaimer:** *confluent with xCAT is currently in beta*
